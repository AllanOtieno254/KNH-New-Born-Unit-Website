Admission Requirements

To ensure safe and timely admission into the Neonatal Intensive Care Unit (NICU), the following conditions and preparations are required.

Medical Referral

Referral from a qualified medical professional or delivery unit.

Baby’s Condition

Preterm birth, respiratory distress, infection risk, or monitoring needs.

Parental Consent

Signed consent for admission, treatment, and emergency procedures.

Availability

NICU bed availability confirmed by the charge nurse.

What to Expect on Arrival

1

Reception check-in and identity verification

2

Initial baby assessment by NICU team

3

Placement in incubator or warming unit

4

Parents briefed on baby’s condition and care plan

Required Documents

Mother’s ID

National ID or passport for verification.

Baby Delivery Records

Birth notes, referral letters, and test results.

Insurance / NHIF

Insurance details or NHIF card if applicable.

Consent Forms

Signed NICU treatment and care consent forms.

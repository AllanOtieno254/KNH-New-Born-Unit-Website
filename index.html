KNH Newborn Unit Overview

The Kenyatta National Hospital Newborn Unit (NBU) is a leading facility dedicated to the care of newborn infants, particularly those who are premature, sick, or require specialized medical attention. Our unit is staffed by a team of experienced healthcare professionals committed to providing the highest quality care for newborns and their families.

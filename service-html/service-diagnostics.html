Monitoring & Diagnostics

Timely tests, continuous monitoring, and precise diagnostic support.

See Diagnostics

Overview

Accurate monitoring and early detection of problems are central to good neonatal outcomes. We use continuous electronic monitoring, point-of-care testing, laboratory diagnostics, and imaging to guide clinical decisions.

Diagnostics & Monitoring Services

  • Continuous cardiorespiratory monitoring (ECG, heart rate, respiratory rate, SpO2)
  • Blood gas analysis and point-of-care testing
  • Microbiology: blood cultures and infection diagnostics
  • Imaging: bedside ultrasound, X-ray, echocardiography referrals
  • Blood chemistry, glucose monitoring, and electrolyte management

FAQs — Diagnostics

Are X-rays safe for newborns?

Imaging is used prudently with shielding and lowest possible exposures; benefits outweigh risks when clinically indicated.

How soon are lab results available?

Certain point-of-care tests return results in minutes; other lab tests may take a few hours depending on sample transport.

Need urgent diagnostic support?

Contact the on-call team via the unit hotline.

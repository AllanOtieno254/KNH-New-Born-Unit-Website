Inside Our Neonatal Facility

A calm, controlled, and highly specialized environment designed to protect fragile newborns while supporting families every step of the way.

Explore the Facility

Purpose-Built for Newborn Care

Our neonatal unit is designed around one principle: delivering advanced medical care while minimizing stress for both babies and parents. The facility layout, lighting, airflow, and workflows are all optimized for safety, infection control, and developmental support.

24/7 Specialist Coverage
Controlled Noise & Lighting
Strict Infection Control
Family-Centered Design

Facility Areas

Main NICU Ward

Open yet controlled space equipped with incubators, radiant warmers, and continuous monitoring systems.

Isolation Rooms

Designed for babies requiring strict infection prevention and specialized airflow control.

Procedure Rooms

Equipped for bedside procedures, minimizing unnecessary movement of critically ill newborns.

Family Support Spaces

Quiet areas for counseling, bonding, and parental education.

Technology That Supports Life

Advanced Incubators

Temperature, humidity, and oxygen-controlled environments.

Ventilators & CPAP

Gentle respiratory support tailored to newborn physiology.

Vital Sign Monitoring

Continuous tracking of heart rate, oxygen, and breathing.

Phototherapy Units

Effective treatment for neonatal jaundice.

Infusion Pumps

Precise delivery of medications and nutrition.

Central Alert Systems

Instant notifications for rapid clinical response.

Supporting Parents Through the Journey

The facility is designed to ensure parents remain close to their baby, receive timely information, and feel supported throughout the NICU stay.

  • Private counseling rooms
  • Breast milk expression areas
  • Education and orientation spaces
  • Comfortable waiting lounges

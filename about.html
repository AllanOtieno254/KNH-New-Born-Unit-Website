About KNH Newborn Unit

Learn more about our mission, vision, and dedicated team providing exceptional newborn care.

Our Mission

To provide specialized, compassionate, and comprehensive care for all newborns,
particularly high-risk and premature infants, while supporting families throughout their journey.

Our Vision

To be world-class neonatal care unit offering the highest quality care,
innovative practices, and family-centered support, ensuring the best possible outcomes for newborns.

Our Values

  • Compassion & Empathy
  • Excellence in Care
  • Innovation & Learning
  • Family-Centered Approach
  • Integrity & Accountability

Why Our Newborn Unit is Unique

Our commitment to excellence makes KNH NBU a leading neonatal care facility.

Advanced Neonatal Equipment

State-of-the-art incubators, monitors & respiratory support for high-risk newborns.

Specialized Medical Team

Highly trained neonatologists, pediatricians, and NICU nurses available 24/7.

Family-Centered Care

We work closely with parents to ensure newborns receive emotional & physical support.

Research & Innovation

Use of modern neonatal research to implement evidence-based practices.

Our Milestones

A journey of growth, excellence, and impact at KNH Newborn Unit.

1995 — Unit Establishment

The KNH Newborn Unit was officially launched to support premature and critically ill infants.

2008 — Major Facility Upgrade

Introduction of advanced neonatal incubators and respiratory support machines.

2015 — Family Care Program

Adoption of Kangaroo Mother Care & enhanced parental involvement policies.

2021 — Digital Monitoring System

Rollout of electronic neonatal monitoring for improved accuracy and real-time data.
